About Us

Our mission is to minimize the mannual work for Blood Bank ,Donar,Blood Group management etc.

In a world where so much improper management of blood and improper communication and synchronization between the blood banks and hospitals leads to wastage of the available blood inventory.

We at Studytonight believe that by automating the existing mannual blood bank management system we can come all the donar,receiver,hospital and blood bank together at one portal and their needs are satisfied.

The aim of our project is to develop an application system to minimize the mannual work for Blood Bank,Donar,Blood Group management etc.
